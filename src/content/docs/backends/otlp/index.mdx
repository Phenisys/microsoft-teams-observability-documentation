The collector supports exporting to any **OTLP-compatible endpoint** (OpenTelemetry Protocol). This enables integration with platforms such as Grafana, Datadog (OTLP ingestion), Jaeger, or any custom OTLP receiver.

## What Is Exported

All standard MS Teams event families are exported as OTLP log records:

- `MSTeams_CallRecords_CallMetadata`
- `MSTeams_CallRecords_StreamDetails`
- `MSTeams_CallRecords_PSTN`
- `MSTeams_CallRecords_DirectRouting`
- `MSTeams_ServiceAnnouncement`
- `MSTeams_CollectionHealth`

## Use Cases

- Forward to a centralised observability platform alongside other telemetry.
- Integrate with existing OTLP pipelines and collectors (OpenTelemetry Collector, Grafana Agent, etc.).
- Bridge to platforms not natively supported by Dynatrace or Splunk integrations.
