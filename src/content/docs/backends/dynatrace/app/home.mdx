> **Path:** `/home` (default landing page)

The Home page is the **observability cockpit** for your Microsoft Teams environment. It provides a high-level summary of call activity and quality over the **last 7 days**. Use it to get a quick pulse check before diving into details on other pages.

There are no filters or controls on this page — all data reflects a fixed 7-day window and is automatically fetched on load.

---

## Layout

The page is organized into two rows:

1. **KPI Cards** — three metric cards side by side
2. **Call Health chart** (left) and **Platforms chart** (right)

---

## KPI Cards

### Call Count

Total number of MS Teams calls recorded in the last 7 days. Shows a **sparkline trend chart** below the number.

### Healthy Calls

Percentage of calls classified as Good out of all calls in the last 7 days.

- **Green** when the percentage is high (healthy)
- **Red** when the percentage is low (degraded)

### Caller Count

Total number of unique callers (participants) who joined at least one call in the last 7 days. Shows a **sparkline trend chart** below the number.

---

## Call Health Chart

A vertical categorical bar chart showing the distribution of calls by health status for the last 7 days.

| Bar | Color | Meaning |
|-----|-------|---------|
| Good | Green | Calls that met quality thresholds |
| Poor | Red | Calls with quality degradation |

---

## Platforms Chart

A pie chart showing the distribution of calls by platform used by participants.

| Slice | Color | Platform |
|-------|-------|----------|
| Windows | Dark green | Windows desktop client |
| macOS | Light blue | macOS desktop client |
| iOS | Purple | iPhone/iPad app |
| Android | Blue | Android app |
| Web | Medium green | Browser-based client |
| Unknown | Light green | Platform could not be determined |

Slices beyond 8 are grouped into an "Other" category automatically. The legend is shown to the right of the pie.

---

## States

| State | What you see |
|-------|-------------|
| Loading | Spinning progress circles in each card/chart |
| No data | "No data available" message inside the chart |
| Data loaded | All metrics and charts rendered |

---

## Navigation

Use the **top navigation bar** to go to Sites, Calls, Users, Issues, or Configuration. This page has no clickable data points that navigate elsewhere.
