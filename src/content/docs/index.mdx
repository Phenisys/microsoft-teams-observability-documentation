## What is MS Teams Observability?

MS Teams Observability is a two-part solution:

- **Collector (`ms-teams-agent`)**: pulls Teams telemetry from Microsoft Graph API and exports to Dynatrace, Splunk, or OpenTelemetry endpoints.
- **Backend integrations**: store and visualise call quality, incidents, site compliance, and user activity in your chosen observability platform.
